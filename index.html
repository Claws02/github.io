About Me

Hello! I'm Caleb, an engineer guided by a core philosophy for my work: every role must satisfy at least two of my three core values: Good People, Meaningful Work, and Good Compensation. This principle steers me towards opportunities where I can collaborate with passionate teams on projects that make a real difference.

My journey started with a childhood fascination for Disney and a spark of inspiration from the talking trash can "PUSH" in the parks, which instilled in me the desire to create things that bring joy and curiosity. This curiosity has evolved into a career focused on innovation and continuous learning. In every role, my goal is not only to apply my existing skills but to constantly expand my knowledge, embracing new challenges that push me to grow as an engineer.

This drive is what led me to my capstone project, where I had the privilege of working with an incredible interdisciplinary team to engineer a drone swarm technology to assist firefighters, a perfect blend of meaningful work and great people.
