About Me

Hello! My name is Caleb, an engineer with a deep passion for designing and making people's lives a little bit more magical. My journey started with a childhood fascination for Disney movies and a spark of inspiration from Iron Man. That spark grew into a habit of taking apart everyday appliances to see how they worked and how I could put them back together even better. This curiosity has evolved into a career focused on innovating and improving the everyday lives of people.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in ElectroMechanical Engineering, where I honed my skills working with interdisciplinary teams to coordinate schedules and mitigate risks. My capstone project involved leading a group to research, design, and engineer a drone swarm technology to assist firefighters. Our system provides autonomous, real-time data on topology, wind, and fire locations to continuously update containment lines, giving first responders a critical advantage in the fight against wildfires.

When I'm not in the lab or workshop, you can find me flying drones, using virtual reality, or camping.
